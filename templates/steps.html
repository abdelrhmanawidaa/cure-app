Your Steps

what diabetaes must do?

please follow this steps

1- Know what to do every day scheduling your day and your meals

2- After early detection and the result is positive, please go to the doctor for follow-up,Take your medicines for diabetes and any other health problems even when you feel good.

3- Check your feet every day for cuts, blisters, red spots, and swelling.

4- Brush your teeth and floss every day to keep your mouth, teeth, and gums healthy.

5- stop smoking and if you are a non-smoker, continue

6- Keep track of your blood sugar

7- Go weigh and diet

9- do exercise, walking









how to Support and deal with a diabetic patient?

please follow this steps

1- Know what diabetes is and how it's treated.

2- Be open to the kind of help they want and don't judge.

3- Look out for the symptoms of low blood sugar.

4- Serve a variety of foods.

5- Be a workout buddy.

6- Offer mental and emotional support.

What should not do with an diabetic patient?

1- Patients who are on insulin or oral hypoglycemic, They should not skip a meal assuming that it can.

2- Do not eat white bread, chips, and pastries, which quickly increase blood sugar.

3- Avoid processed foods and meats as they will be rich in salt and oil.

4- Restrict fried and fatty foods.









Symptoms of a diabetic coma

- Symptoms of high or low blood sugar usually appear before a diabetic coma:

If your blood sugar level is too high, you may experience:

extreme thirst
frequent urination
Blurred vision
tiredness or weakness
Headache
Nausea and vomiting
shortness of breath
stomachache
Fruity breath smell
Very dry mouth
Low blood sugar (hypoglycemia) .

If your blood sugar level is too low, you may experience:

Shivering
anxiety
tiredness or drowsiness
Weakness
sweating
hunger
Feeling of tingling in the skin
dizziness or lightheadedness
Headache
difficulty speaking
Blurry vision
confusion
Unconsciousness .
